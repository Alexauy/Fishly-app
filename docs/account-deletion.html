Account Deletion

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Fishly allows users to delete their account and associated stored data.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open Fishly
  2. Go to the Profile page
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm the request and enter your password when prompted

Delete Your Account Without Accessing the App

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing alexauyon2017@gmail.com.

What Data Is Deleted

When an account deletion request is completed, Fishly deletes:

  • Your account record
  • Your planner goals and subgoals
  • Your completion history
  • Your coins, owned items, and equipped items

Contact
