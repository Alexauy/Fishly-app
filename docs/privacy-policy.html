Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fishly collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Fishly mobile application.

Information We Collect

Fishly may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Account information such as your email address and display name
  • Planner information such as goals, subgoals, and goal details
  • App progress information such as completion history and coins
  • Personalization information such as owned items and equipped items

How We Use Information

Fishly uses this information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and sync your planner data across sessions
  • Track progress, rewards, and monthly history
  • Save personalization choices such as Gubby accessories and tank decorations
  • Support account management and deletion requests

How Information Is Stored

Fishly uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore, to store account and app data. These third-party services process data on behalf of Fishly so the app can function.

Data Sharing

Fishly does not sell your personal information. Information is only shared with service providers needed to operate the app, such as Firebase.

Data Retention and Deletion

Fishly keeps your data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide the app’s features. You can request deletion of your account and associated planner data at any time.

You can delete your data in either of these ways:

  • Use the in-app account deletion option available on the Profile page
  • Email a deletion request to alexauyon2017@gmail.com

Security

Fishly uses reasonable technical measures and trusted third-party infrastructure to protect stored data. However, no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Fishly is not directed to children under 13, and the app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to make a privacy-related request, contact: alexauyon2017@gmail.com
